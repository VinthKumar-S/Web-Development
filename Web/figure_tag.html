HORSE BREEDS


        The Arabian horse has contributed its qualities to most of the 
        modern breeds of light horses.

        Arabian horse being guided up a steep mountain slope during an 
        endurance race.

        The Arabian breed is a compact, relatively small horse with a 
        small head,protruding eyes, wide nostrils, marked withers, 
        and a short back. 

        It usually has only 23 vertebrae, while 24 is the usual 
        number for other breeds.
